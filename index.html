Chefs-Hub: Top 20 Dishes (2016-2026)

Molecular Apple Sphere

Apparatus
  • Digital Scale
  • Spherification Spoon
  • Immersion Blender
Recipe & Steps
  1. Mix Apple juice with Calcium Lactate.
  2. Drop mixture into a Sodium Alginate bath.
  3. Rinse in water and serve on a ceramic spoon.

Nitro-Poached Basil Mousse

Apparatus
  • Liquid Nitrogen Tank
  • Siphon Whipper
  • Cryo-gloves
Recipe & Steps
  1. Charge basil cream in a siphon.
  2. Express foam into a bowl of Liquid Nitrogen.
  3. Freeze for 10 seconds until a "shell" forms.
