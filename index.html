01. Smoked Makhani Sphere & Saffron Paneer

A precision study in vacuum-infusion and aromatic smoke suspension.

Vacuum Sealer Hickory Smoker Siphon Whipper (N2O) Sous-Vide Immersion
01
Select premium malai paneer and cut into exact 30mm cubes using a precision blade.
02
Infuse 100ml of warm milk with Kashmiri saffron strands for 30 minutes.
03
Vacuum-seal paneer with saffron infusion to force flavor into the protein core.
04
Sous-vide the paneer at 65°C for 20 minutes for a butter-soft texture.
05
Roast Roma tomatoes at 200°C until skins char and natural sugars caramelize.
06
Blend roasted tomatoes with ginger-garlic; strain through a fine-mesh chinois.
07
Emulsify the sauce with cold unsalted butter for a high-gloss professional finish.
08
Infuse Hickory smoke into the sauce using a handheld smoker for 5 minutes.
09
Add 2g Soy Lecithin to the smoked sauce to ensure foam stability.
10
Charge siphon with two N2O cartridges and maintain temperature at 50°C.
11
Dehydrate black garlic and mix with roasted cumin to create a "Coal Crumb."
12
Flash-sear one side of the paneer with a kitchen torch for tandoori aroma.
13
Position the paneer cube centrally on a chilled stone ceramic plate.
14
Gently express the Smoked Makhani Foam around the base of the paneer.
15
Garnish with Coal Crumb and micro-greens using plating tweezers. Serve.
